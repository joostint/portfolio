About Me!

I'm a UI/UX designer originally from Cleveland, Ohio, but moved to Columbus to pursue my education at The Ohio State University. Currently in my fourth year, I'm double majoring in Communication, specializing in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) and User Experience (UX), and Psychology.

Outside of academics, I'm passionate about staying up to date with the latest trends and advancements in technology. Whether it's exploring new design tools or reading about AI innovations, I'm always eager to expand my knowledge and skills in a rapidly evolving tech field.

In my leisure time, you'll often find me partaking in a variety of hobbies. I love building custom mechanical keyboards. I find joy in the high personalization and the meticulous process of selecting key switches, and keycaps, and designing layouts. When I'm not at my desk, I love to hit the streets on my skateboard. And on weekends, you might catch me casting lines at a nearby pond.
